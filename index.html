Microsoft Purview Services

comprehensive set of solutions that can help your organization govern, protect, and manage data, wherever it lives.

Data Map

Automatically discovers and catalogs your data assets across on-prem, cloud, and SaaS — creating a searchable inventory.

Access Review

Regularly checks who has access to sensitive data and helps remove unnecessary permissions.

Data Lifecycle Management (Retention Labels & Policies)

Controls how long data is kept or deleted to stay compliant with regulations and reduce storage risks.

Data Loss Prevention (DLP)

Detects and blocks sensitive data (like PII or financial info) from being accidentally shared or leaked.

Data Catalog

Lets users easily find and understand data using business-friendly search, filters, and classifications.

Information Protection & Auto-Labelling

Automatically applies sensitivity labels to documents and emails based on their content to enforce protection rules.
